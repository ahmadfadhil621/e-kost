# Requirements — Room Available Card Cleanup (issue #103)

## Acceptance Criteria

- AC-1: An available room card in the room list shows room number, room type/rent, and the "Available" status badge only.
- AC-2: An available room card does NOT render "Assign Tenant" or "Change Status" action buttons or links.
- AC-3: Clicking an available room card navigates to the Room Detail page (unchanged).
- AC-4: Occupied and under_renovation card variants are unaffected.
- AC-5: The full-card link meets the 44×44px minimum touch target.
- AC-6: Mobile viewport (320px–480px) shows no horizontal scroll.
